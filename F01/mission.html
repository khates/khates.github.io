Mission

Mission

Our mission is to inspire through sport, unite humanity, and promote peace and sustainability by upholding the highest standards of ethics, governance, and inclusivity in the Olympic Movement.

  1. To encourage and support the promotion of ethics and good governance in sport, and to educate youth through sport with a commitment to fair play and banning violence.
  2. To organize, develop, and coordinate sports competitions, ensuring the regular celebration of the Olympic Games.
  3. To cooperate with organizations and authorities to place sport at the service of humanity, promoting peace and unity.
  4. To strengthen the unity and independence of the Olympic Movement while maintaining political neutrality and autonomy in sport.
  5. To combat discrimination in all its forms within the Olympic Movement.
  6. To encourage and support the promotion of women in sport at all levels, ensuring equality between men and women.
  7. To protect clean athletes and the integrity of sport by leading the fight against doping and corruption.
  1. To ensure the health and medical care of athletes, promoting safety and protection from harassment and abuse.
  2. To support athletes' social and professional future, ensuring their well-being beyond sports.
  3. To advocate for sustainable development in sport, promoting environmental responsibility and leaving a positive legacy for host cities and regions.
  4. To blend sport with culture and education, supporting initiatives like the International Olympic Academy (IOA) for Olympic education.
  5. To oppose any political or commercial misuse of sport and athletes.
  6. To promote sport for all, ensuring inclusivity and accessibility for every individual.
  7. To encourage and support measures fostering innovation, legacy, and sustainability in the Olympic Games.
